About SIPRS

Founded after the July Uprising 2025, SIPRS is a non-partisan research institute dedicated to data-driven analysis of Bangladesh's political landscape, economic trends, agricultural development, public health policies, technological advancements, and environmental challenges.

In collaboration with Sonicium Ltd, we provide strategic insights that shape policy discussions and political understanding, bridging the gap between academic research and practical implementation.

Learn More About Us

Hussain Billah

Founder & Chief Analyst, SIPRS

A visionary in political analysis and digital strategy, Hussain Billah established SIPRS to create a platform for rigorous, non-partisan political and economic research in Bangladesh. With over a decade of experience in political consulting and digital campaign strategy, Billah brings a unique perspective to policy analysis.

As a senior executive at Sonicium Ltd, he has pioneered data-driven approaches to understanding political behavior and economic trends, with the goal of fostering more informed policy decisions in Bangladesh.
